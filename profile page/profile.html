Rita Kirshstein

Rita’s work in higher education covers many different areas. While at the American Institutes for Research for many years, she directed the Delta Cost Project that focused on college affordability through the lens of college spending and studied numerous higher education initiatives. Currently, she is a Visiting Professor of Higher Education at George Washington University (half-time), provides college counseling through her business, College Connections, LLC, and serves on the board of College Bound, a college access program for DC-area public and charter school students. Follow her on Twitter: @RitaKirshstein
